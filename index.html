Get in touch

If you have a support issue or would like to contact the developer about an issue, idea or feature, you can follow one of the below steps:

  1. Click send an email
  2. Open the app, tap the (i) button in the top right corner and scroll to the section "Support". This will open your preferred email app on your device.
  3. Send an email to seaniosdeveloper@gmail.com

If you are emailing about an issue, please ensure you provide the app version details, which can be found on the App Store or in the app in the Support section.
